## Proprietor

**CMA Samar Dhar** — M.Com, FCMA

CMA Samar Dhar is a cost accountant who passed out in the year 1997. Besides being a cost accountant, he holds an M.Com and CA (Inter) degree, obtained in 1995 and 1997 respectively. He started his career in the year 2000 and joined Ray & Ray, a Chartered Accountant firm, as an audit assistant.

After that, he joined HAL in the year 2003 as a cost accountant and took part in material procurement including tender, bill processing, and costing in the cost centre. In the year 2004, he started his own firm and began his career in a new dimension as a cost and management accountant.

He has vast knowledge in income tax, GST, company and other registrations, all statutory compliances, PF, ESI, P.Tax and other allied matters. Project report preparation and investment consultancy are two expertise areas of Mr. Dhar. Internal audit and other audits are also domains of Mr. Dhar. Moreover, he has a vast experience of 22 years in the field of direct and indirect taxes — namely individual and corporate income taxation, GST and Excise matters. He is also an experienced person in handling appeals and tribunal cases in various areas of taxes i.e. VAT, GST and Income Tax.
